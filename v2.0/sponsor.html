Strengthening the Society

BuildIT Society was created to connect students with similar interests in building exciting fun tech. With the help of our sponsors, we now have expanded this community beyond our campus. For the first time, our members have direct access to professional engineers in Industry to network with and ask questions. We at BuildIT Society, invision a future in which undergradutes in Academia and working professionals in Industry can come together to strengthen the society of builders and creators.
