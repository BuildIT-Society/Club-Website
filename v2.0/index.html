BuildIT Society

Build. Ignite. Transform.

Building Communities of Creators

BuildIT Society was created to connect students with similar interests in building exciting fun tech. Our club culture is one of inclusion and collabortion. We believe the best learning and innovation happens when students work together in tightly-knit groups. At our core, we are groups of individuals that work together to solve and create projects we find interesting.

Igniting Passion in Students

At BuildIT Society, we give students an expressive outlet to their ideas and creations that might otherwise go unnoticed. The type of individuals that are involved in the club are enthusiastic about creating new tech and willing to learn new skills. Although students may get project experience in class, our club lets them take their ideas and pursue them over a whole semester without the extra overhead that typically comes with class projects.

Transforming Individuals

When BuildIT Society started, one of our main goals was allowing students to get real-world experience that they might not get in the classroom setting. Many courses go very in-depth into the theoretical side of the sciences and engineering, but they don't always offer great hands-on experience that is sought after in Industry. Our goal as a club, is to let students work on a project that challenges and teaches them something, while applying what they learn in the classroom to real-life situations.
