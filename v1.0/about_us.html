About Us

There’s a clear gap between what students know and what students can do.
More often than not, real life experience and practical abilities mark
the difference between getting the internship of your dreams or just another tedious
resume-builder job. We seek to narrow that gap by getting our members involved with
unique projects that they’re genuinely excited about. We realize that organizations
that build cool things are abound, but oftentimes, they’re focused on a single area,
or are so big that you end up contributing close to nothing on a certain
project that you’re involved in. BuildIT Society teams will never be comprised of more
than 10 members, and will always be focused on a project chosen via a majority of votes.
