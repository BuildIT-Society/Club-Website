Resources Available to the Club

Boiler Maker Lab

Located in Knoy Hall of Technology

Click here to visit their site.

What is it?

The Boiler Maker Lab is a 3-D printing lab open to all Purdue students.
The lab has multiple printers available and all Purdue students
get a weekly quota of free printing material.

How can I use it?

To start using the 3-D lab, you need to first make an account.
The details of how to make an account are on their site.
Once you have created an account, then you just need to upload
your stl file for printing and follow their instructions for printing.
After the print is complete, all you need to do is pick it up from the lab!

Artisan and Fabrication Lab

Located in Armstrong Hall of Engineering

Click here to visit their site.

What is it?

The Artisan and Fabrication Lab, (aka AFL lab), is a lab
intended to allow students an area to work on their school projects
or personal projects. The lab offers many tools to work
with metals and wood for projects.

How can I use it?

To start using the AFL lab, you need to first make an account and submit a project to work on.
The details of how to make an account are on their site.
Once you have created an account, you need to complete the online
safety training for the specific machines you intend on using for your project
The lab has TA's on hand that can help with projects, but make
sure to bring your own pair of safety glasses when you work in the lab.
